The Celebration

Join us to welcome the arrival of spring at an intimate Newroz celebration in one of Notting Hill’s most iconic venues.

Expect Kurdish music throughout the night, spontaneous halparke, and a crowd bringing great energy and shared celebration.

This is not a commercial club night. It is a private cultural gathering.

Music & Atmosphere

The evening will feature a mix of traditional Kurdish tracks and contemporary party music, creating space for cultural expression and pure celebration.

Dancing begins early and continues through to 2am.

Dress

Traditional Kurdish dress is warmly encouraged, but not required. Smart evening wear is equally welcome.

Practical Information

Venue: Notting Hill Arts Club, W11 3JQ

Time: 20:00 – 02:00

Tickets: £25 per person (venue hire contribution)

Tickets are strictly limited and will not be available at the door. Once capacity is reached, sales will close.
