The Celebration

Join us to welcome the arrival of spring at an intimate Newroz celebration at Notting Hill Arts Club.

Expect Kurdish music throughout the night, spontaneous halparke, and a crowd bringing great energy and shared celebration.

This is not a commercial club night. It is a private cultural gathering.

Music & Atmosphere

A mix of traditional Kurdish tracks and contemporary party music will carry the night from 8pm through to 2am.

Dress

Traditional Kurdish dress is warmly encouraged, though not required. Smart evening wear is equally welcome.
